Step 1

Install Wrrapd’s Chrome extension

If you haven’t already, install the free Wrrapd Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store using one of the install links on this page. Once it’s on, Wrrapd appears when you shop and checkout at our supported retailers—Amazon, Target, Walmart, Nordstrom, Kohl’s, Ulta, Sephora, Best Buy, LEGO, and Etsy.

Step 2

Shop as usual, then choose Wrrapd at checkout

Add gifts to your cart on any supported store and check out like you always do. When Wrrapd’s gift-wrap option appears in cart or checkout, select it to start your wrap order—same retailer account, same familiar flow.

Step 3

Customize wrap, AI art, and flowers

In the Wrrapd modal, choose how you want the gift wrapped—let Wrrapd pick, upload your own art, or generate designs with AI. Add an optional bouquet before you continue.

Step 4

Confirm shipping to the Wrrapd hub

On the store’s shipping screens, follow Wrrapd’s prompts and ship the order to the Wrrapd hub so we can receive it, wrap it professionally, and send the finished gift to your recipient.

Step 5

Pay Wrrapd and finish store checkout

Use the Wrrapd Summary to Pay Wrrapd for wrapping and any add-ons, then complete the retailer’s checkout for the products themselves—two payments, one beautifully wrapped delivery.
