Our story

Wrapping happiness—one gift at a time

At Wrrapd, gifting isn’t only what’s inside the box—it’s the moment someone unwraps it. We combine thoughtful gift-wrap, optional flowers, and AI-assisted personalization so every present feels as unique as the occasion.

Our mission

We make gift-giving feel effortless and intentional. Birthdays, holidays, milestones, or just-because—Wrrapd helps your gift arrive with style and sentiment, without the tape-and-scissors scramble.

What we do

  • Custom gift-wrap designs — AI-assisted patterns and uploads tailored to the recipient and occasion.
  • Seamless checkout add-on — Shop at supported retailers, choose Wrrapd at checkout, and we handle professional exterior wrapping (and optional flowers).
  • Delivery coordination — Hub intake, quality control, and outbound shipping so the unboxing matches the care you put into picking the gift.
