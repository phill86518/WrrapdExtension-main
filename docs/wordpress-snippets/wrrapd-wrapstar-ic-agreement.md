## 1. Parties

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is between **Wrrapd, LLC** ("Wrrapd") and the undersigned individual ("WrapStar" or "Contractor").

## 2. Relationship

WrapStar is an **independent contractor**, not an employee, partner, or agent of Wrrapd. WrapStar controls how, when, and where services are performed, subject to Wrrapd quality standards.

## 3. Services

WrapStar will:

- Receive retailer packages at WrapStar's designated mailing address / PO Box
- Unbox, inspect, and professionally gift-wrap items per Wrrapd specifications
- Record **video proof** on every order: (a) package receipt/unboxing, (b) wrapping process, (c) finished gift, (d) handoff to outbound carrier
- Ship wrapped gifts to recipients using Wrrapd-provided labels or instructions

## 4. Video proof (non-negotiable)

WrapStar agrees that **video proof is required on every order** without exception. Failure to provide complete video proof may result in immediate suspension or termination.

## 5. Insurance requirements

Before activation and at all times during this Agreement, WrapStar must maintain:

1. **Commercial General Liability** insurance with limits of **not less than $1,000,000** per occurrence
2. **Inland Marine / Bailee** or equivalent coverage for customer goods in WrapStar's possession

WrapStar will provide certificates of insurance upon request and notify Wrrapd within 10 days of cancellation or material change.

## 6. Personal liability & indemnification

WrapStar is **personally responsible** for loss, theft, damage, or destruction of customer goods while in WrapStar's possession, custody, or control, except to the extent caused by Wrrapd's gross negligence.

WrapStar **indemnifies, defends, and holds harmless** Wrrapd, its officers, directors, and affiliates from any claims, damages, losses, liabilities, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:

- WrapStar's performance or failure to perform under this Agreement
- Loss, theft, or damage to customer goods in WrapStar's possession
- WrapStar's violation of law or this Agreement
- Any injury or property damage caused by WrapStar

## 7. Performance standards & tiering

- New WrapStars may receive **lower-value orders** until performance is established
- Wrrapd may **deactivate** WrapStars for missing video proof, poor quality, customer complaints, or ratings below Wrrapd standards
- WrapStar must respond to Wrrapd communications within 24 hours during active order periods

## 8. Confidentiality

WrapStar will not disclose customer information, gift contents, addresses, or Wrrapd operational details to third parties.

## 9. Term & termination

Either party may terminate at any time with written notice. Wrrapd may terminate immediately for breach of video, insurance, or indemnification obligations.

## 10. Governing law

Florida law governs this Agreement. Disputes shall be resolved in courts located in Duval County, Florida.

## 11. Entire agreement

This Agreement, together with Wrrapd policies communicated to WrapStar, constitutes the entire agreement.
