## Module 1: What is a WrapStar?

You are an independent contractor in Wrrapd's gift-wrapping network. Customers shop at major retailers; packages ship to **your** PO Box or mailing address. You wrap professionally and ship to the final recipient.

You are **not** a Wrrapd employee. You control your schedule, but must meet Wrrapd quality and proof standards.

## Module 2: Video proof (every order)

**Non-negotiable.** For every order you must upload or submit video showing:

1. **Receipt** — package as received (label visible if safe)
2. **Unboxing** — opening the retailer package
3. **Wrapping** — your wrap process
4. **Finished gift** — completed exterior wrap
5. **Outbound handoff** — package handed to USPS/UPS/FedEx

Missing video proof is grounds for **immediate suspension**.

> Placeholder video: `https://wrrapd.com/wp-content/uploads/wrapstars/orientation-01-video-proof.mp4`

## Module 3: Insurance

You must carry before activation:

| Coverage | Minimum |
|----------|---------|
| General Liability | $1,000,000 per occurrence |
| Inland Marine / Bailee | Covers customer goods in your possession |

Upload your COI during onboarding. Keep coverage active — lapse = deactivation.

## Module 4: Liability & indemnification

While customer goods are in your possession, **you are personally responsible** for loss, theft, or damage. The IC Agreement requires you to **indemnify Wrrapd** for claims arising from your work.

## Module 5: Performance & tiering

- **New WrapStars** start with **lower-value orders**
- Build your rating with complete video proof and on-time shipping
- **2+ missed videos** or **rating below 4.0** → review and possible deactivation
- Wrrapd may suspend or deactivate WrapStars at any time for policy violations

## Module 6: PO Box & package handling

- Use only the mailing address approved during onboarding
- Inspect packages on receipt; report damage immediately
- Never open gifts beyond what video proof requires
- Ship only via carriers and labels Wrrapd provides or approves
