WRAPSTAR CODE OF CONDUCT

AND PLATFORM GUIDELINES

Wrrapd, Inc. — WrapStar Independent Contractor Network (Gift Wrapping Only · WrapStar App) Companion document to the WrapStar Independent Contractor Technology Services Agreement (the “Services Agreement”)

This Code of Conduct and Platform Guidelines (the “Code”) sets behavioral and professionalism expectations for WrapStar independent contractors who perform gift-wrapping only using the WrapStar App and related Platform tools. Delivery and driving are outside the WrapStar role. Legal terms governing compensation, custody of goods, intellectual property, confidentiality, App license, and dispute resolution are set forth in the Services Agreement and other Companion Documents. In the event of conflict, the Services Agreement controls on commercial and legal terms; this Code controls on day-to-day conduct standards unless Wrrapd publishes a more specific policy.

By signing, Contractor agrees to follow this Code and Wrrapd’s published Platform / WrapStar App standards, as updated from time to time with notice via the WrapStar App, Platform, or email.

1. Professionalism and Respect

1.1 Treat Wrrapd personnel, couriers or carriers who pick up staged orders (if you encounter them), and any other persons you meet in connection with wrapping work with courtesy and respect.

1.2 Maintain a clean, organized wrapping workspace.

1.3 Do not engage in discrimination, harassment, threats, abusive language, or any conduct that undermines safety or Wrrapd’s reputation.

2. Quality and Brand Standards

2.1 Wrap all gifts to Wrrapd brand and quality standards, using appropriate materials and care for the item type.

2.2 Follow order-specific instructions, including designs, messages, flower add-ons, and timing windows published in the Platform.

2.3 Do not substitute materials or alter designs in a way that materially departs from Wrrapd standards without Platform approval.

3. WrapStar App, Proof, and Honesty

3.1 Use the WrapStar App as your primary tool for order offers, status updates, messages, and proof uploads. Keep the App reasonably up to date and do not share your login.

3.2 Record and upload clear, authentic proof for every order as required by the Services Agreement—typically unboxing/inspection, wrapping process, and finished gift. You are not required to video-record receipt from or handoff to a driver.

3.3 Never fabricate, edit misleadingly, reuse proof from another order, or misrepresent order status or completion in the App.

3.4 Update order status truthfully and promptly in the WrapStar App, including when an order is ready for pickup or release. Do not use bots, scripts, or other unauthorized automation.

4. Custody and Care of Goods

4.1 Safeguard all customer merchandise while in your possession. Do not leave packages unsecured or accessible to unauthorized persons.

4.2 Do not open packages beyond what is reasonably necessary to inspect, wrap, and create required proof.

4.3 Never use, consume, gift, sell, or keep customer merchandise or flowers for personal purposes.

4.4 Report damage, shortage, suspected tampering, or loss to Wrrapd promptly in accordance with the Services Agreement and Platform instructions.

5. Confidentiality and Privacy (Conduct Reminder)

5.1 Do not discuss, post, or share gift contents, addresses, customer information, or order details (including screenshots from the WrapStar App) with anyone not authorized to receive them.

5.2 Do not photograph or film people or private spaces beyond what WrapStar App wrapping proof requires.

5.3 Full confidentiality and data-use obligations appear in the Services Agreement and survive termination.

6. Brand, Social Media, and Public Statements

6.1 Do not use Wrrapd’s name, logos, or marks in advertising, social media, or fundraising except as Wrrapd expressly authorizes.

6.2 Do not speak to the press or public as a representative of Wrrapd without prior written authorization.

6.3 Personal social posts must not disclose Confidential Information or depict customer gifts in a way that identifies the recipient without authorization.

7. Compliance and Cooperation

7.1 Comply with all applicable laws and with Wrrapd’s onboarding, screening, and tax requirements.

7.2 Cooperate reasonably with Wrrapd investigations of quality, loss, or customer complaints.

7.3 Respond to Wrrapd communications (including in-App notifications and messages) within published timeframes when you have active or assigned orders or have indicated availability in the App.

8. Prohibited Conduct (Non-Exhaustive)

The following may result in immediate suspension or removal from the Platform and termination of the Services Agreement:

  • Discrimination, harassment, violence, or threats;
  • Theft, conversion, or personal appropriation of customer goods;
  • Failure to provide required proof, or submission of false proof;
  • Sharing WrapStar App credentials or allowing others to operate your account;
  • Chronic poor quality, missed timeframes, or ratings below Wrrapd’s published standards;
  • Unauthorized disclosure of customer or order information;
  • Circumventing the Platform or misusing the WrapStar App in violation of the Services Agreement; or
  • Any conduct that materially harms customers or Wrrapd’s reputation or operations.

9. Enforcement

9.1 Wrrapd may counsel, suspend, deactivate, or terminate access for violations of this Code or published Platform standards.

9.2 Performance thresholds (including proof completeness and quality ratings) may be published in orientation materials or the Platform and may change prospectively. Repeated missed proof or sustained ratings below published standards may trigger review and deactivation.

9.3 Nothing in this Code alters Contractor’s independent-contractor status under the Services Agreement. Wrrapd does not require commercial insurance as a condition of engagement under the Services Agreement.
