PART A — DISCLOSURE

(Standalone Fair Credit Reporting Act Notice)

Wrrapd, Inc. (“Wrrapd”) may obtain one or more consumer reports and/or investigative consumer reports about you for use in connection with your application to become a WrapStar independent contractor (gift-wrapping services), access to the WrapStar App, and, if engaged, for purposes related to your continued engagement, including assignment eligibility, trust and safety, and compliance.

Consumer reports and investigative consumer reports may include information concerning your:

  • Criminal history;
  • Identity verification;
  • Prior addresses and other public-record information; and
  • Other information bearing on your character, general reputation, personal characteristics, or mode of living, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

An investigative consumer report is a consumer report in which information about character, general reputation, personal characteristics, or mode of living is obtained through personal interviews with neighbors, friends, associates, or others. If Wrrapd requests an investigative consumer report, you have the right, upon written request made within a reasonable time, to receive additional disclosures regarding the nature and scope of the investigation and a summary of your rights under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (“FCRA”).

Wrrapd may obtain such reports before you begin providing services and periodically during your engagement, consistent with applicable law.

Note: The WrapStar role under the current Services Agreement is gift wrapping only and does not include driving or delivery. Wrrapd does not routinely obtain motor vehicle records for WrapStars in that role. If your role later expands to include driving under a separate agreement, Wrrapd may provide an updated disclosure before obtaining driving records.

Wrrapd will use a third-party consumer reporting agency (the “Agency”). When Wrrapd designates an Agency, Wrrapd will provide the Agency’s name, address, and telephone number in the Platform, onboarding materials, or a supplemental notice. You may contact the Agency regarding your report as permitted by the FCRA.

A summary of your rights under the FCRA is available from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (or successor agency) and will be provided as required by law (including with any pre-adverse or adverse action notice).

This Part A is a disclosure only. It does not authorize Wrrapd to obtain reports. Your authorization appears in Part B below.

PART B — AUTHORIZATION

I have read and understand Part A — Disclosure.

I hereby authorize Wrrapd, Inc. and its designated consumer reporting agency(ies) to obtain consumer reports and/or investigative consumer reports about me, including criminal background information and identity-related information, to the extent permitted by applicable law:

  1. In connection with my application and onboarding as a WrapStar independent contractor; and
  2. Periodically during my engagement, as permitted by applicable law, for continued eligibility, trust and safety, and compliance purposes.

I authorize all persons, schools, employers, law-enforcement agencies, and other entities to release information about me to Wrrapd’s designated Agency for these purposes.

I understand that:

  • My engagement, activation on the Platform / WrapStar App, and/or continued access to orders may be conditioned on the results of such reports, consistent with applicable law;
  • If Wrrapd intends to take adverse action based in whole or in part on a consumer report, I will receive any notices required by the FCRA (including a copy of the report and a summary of rights, as applicable) before a final adverse decision;
  • I may dispute inaccurate or incomplete information with the Agency as provided by the FCRA; and
  • This authorization remains in effect during my application and engagement unless revoked in writing, except that Wrrapd may retain reports and related records as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

I certify that the identifying information I provide for screening (including full legal name, date of birth, addresses, and government identification as requested) is true and complete.

I consent to background checks and lawful ongoing monitoring as a condition of my application and engagement as a WrapStar.
